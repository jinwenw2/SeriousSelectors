Rules of Checker

  • Alternate turns between each player
  • Pieces may only be moved diagonally
  • If your piece is next to an opposing piece and there is a space to jump over the opposing piece, do so to take it!
  • Pieces cannot be moved backwards unless they are King pieces
  • Pieces can be turned into Kings when moved to the edge of opposing side
  • Win by taking all of your opponents pieces!
